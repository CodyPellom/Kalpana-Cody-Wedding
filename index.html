We are getting married!

Kalpana Arora & Cody Pellom are happy to annouce they are getting married on December 24th, 2018! Find out more below!

About Us

Kalpana and Cody met in the summer of 2015. They started off as best friends and eventually fell in love. They have been together ever since and decided to take their relationship to the next level with marriage.

Join Us

The wedding will be taking place on December 24th at The Roseate in New Delhi, India. Join us for food, fun and dancing!

Ceremony and Events

There are a number of events planned before the ceremony.

The Henna Ceremony (AKA Mendhi) will take place on December 23rd. The bride, family and friends will be pampered and decorated with beautiful henna body art. The groom will also be decorated with henna and tumeric paste.

The Sangeet is a cocktail party & rehersal dinner hosted the night before the wedding. There will be food, drinks, dancing and good music all night long!

The Wedding will take place at the Roseate. The Roseate is a beautiful resort tucked away from the hustle bustle of Delhi. The bride and groom will be married in the presence of family and frineds.

VIP Section

Below are a few of the people who are helping to make the wedding possible. To find out more about them, click on their picture!

Kunal Arora is Kalpana's younger brother. He has always been an integral part of the family & will be joining the wedding alongside the couple to assist with various tasks throughout the wedding.

Rupesh & Neelima Arora are Kalpana's loving parents. They are two of the most supportive and loving people. They have been an integral part of planning and preparing for the wedding.

Aditi Sahi is Kalpana's younger cousin who is is an artist and a student in New Delhi. She will be serving as a bridesmaid for the wedding ceremony and will be helping with decorations.

Rupal Singh is a childhood friend of Kalpana. Rupal is an engineer and entrepreneur who resides in New Delhi. Rupal will be serving as a bridesmaid.

Puneet Sahi is Kalpana’s cousin and will be the ring bearer in addition to helping with the wedding. Puneet is an engineering student in New Delhi. His hobbies include playing guitar and sports.
